About

SQLiteManager is a tool for managing time in a work/break cycle pattern. Break down periods of work into 25-minute intervals separated by short breaks. Try to reduce the impact of internal and external interruptions, and you will get an efficient day!

You can use SQLiteManager to add pomos and breaks. There is also a project-based pomo management system to help you to track where you spent your time.
